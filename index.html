A little about me...

I'm a professional graphic designer and illustrator with over ten years of experience currently residing in Madison, WI. My aesthetic tends towards clean lines, inviting colors, and overall less-is-more mentality. I graduated from Miami University (Oxford Ohio) with a Bachelor's degree of Arts in Architecture. After college I worked in the architectural field for roughly two years, but ultimately decided to pursue a career in graphic design and illustration which has allowed me to more fully utilize my creative skills. Through my experience in both fields, I have developed a wide skillset that can bring a unique and fresh perspective to your team or project.

Graphic Design

Posters, booklets, brochures, infographics and icons are just a few types of collateral I have worked on. I am highly skilled with Adobe InDesign, Adobe Acrobat, and Scribus. Additionally, I am very familiar with how to set up files for print production and have had much experience working with professional printers to see projects through production.
