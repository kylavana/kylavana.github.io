My background

I’m a graphic/web designer and illustrator with over six years of experience working in Madison Wisconsin. My aesthetic tends towards clean lines, inviting colors and overall less-is-more mentality. I graduated from Miami University (Oxford Ohio) with a Bachelor's degree of Arts in Architecture. After College, I worked in the architectural field for over two years, but ultimately decided to pursue a career in graphic/web design which has allowed me to more fully utilize my creative skills. Through my experience in both fields, I have developed a wide skillset that can bring a unique and fresh perspective to your team or project.

Skillset

Web Development

With over 6 years of experience in front end development, I am fluent with HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I have experience working with the jQuery and Angular libraries, as well as CSS preprocessors like SCSS. Additionally, I am familiar with php and sql databases.

Illustration

I have done professional illustrations for nearly 7 years. My work includes everything from detailed technical illustrations to icons and pixel artwork. I am very experienced with Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrators, GIMP, and Inkscape.

Graphic Design

Posters, booklets, brochures, and infographics are just a few types of collateral I have worked on. I am skilled with Adobe InDesign, Adobe Acrobat, and Scribus. Additionally, I have experience working with professional printers to see projects through production.

Additional Skills

Throughout my career, I have developed several additional skills including: Animation (web and flash), 3D modeling (Sketchup) and video editing (Adobe Premier). I am also skilled with traditional media such as charcoal and watercolor.
