I.About

Dungeons & Javascript (D&J) is a simple text-based dungeon crawling game with some rogue like features. The goal is to get through as many rooms as possible. Like many rogue likes, D&J cannnot be "beaten" and character death is permanent.

II.Abilities

Strength(Str) determines you damage and chance to hit with melee attacks. It is also used for forcing doors and chests open.

Dexerity(Dex) determines you damage and chance to hit with ranged attacks. It is also used for picking locks.

Intelligence(Int) determines your maximum amount of spellpoints, and how powerful your spells are.

Consitution(Con) determines your maximum amount of hitpoints.

III.Character Race

Humans: no ability bonus or penalty

Dwarves: +2 Con, -2 Dex

Elves: +2 Int, -2 Con

Halfling: +2 Dex, -2 Str

Orcs: +2 Str, -2 Int

IV.Character Class

Fighters recieve the largest bonus to attack per level, have highest starting hitpoints, and may use any armor or weapons. They cannot use spells.

Mages recieve the largest increase to spellpoints per level, but cannot use martial weapons or armor. They start with the most spellpoints but the fewest hitpoints

Rogues recieve a moderate bonus to their attack and spellpoints per level. They cannot use martial weapons or heavy armor. Rogues also get a bonus to their AC.
